SELECT
Climbing Mortality Models

Misurina (BL) - Italy.
August 30 - September 2, 2022. [Bando MIUR PRIN 2017]

Di User <CC BY-SA 3.0, Collegamento

Venue

Misurina is located at the North of Italy, within the Dolomites (see map).

A Shuttle service from and to Venice Airport will be provided for those who make request. If you want to book your seat on shuttle service please fill in this form.

The workshop will take place at the Grand Hotel Misurina.

Program

This workshop aims at creating the bases for a fruitful collaboration among statisticians and demographers in terms of future research directions on mortality modelling and forecasting.
