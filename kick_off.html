SELECT kick-off meeting

Ca' Foscari University, Venice. 14-15 October, 2019.
Campus San Giobbe, Ca' Foscari University, Aula Partesotti. [Bando MIUR PRIN 2017]

Program

This first meeting aims at creating the bases for a fruitful collaboration among the different units in terms of future research directions, discussions of relevant datasets, presentation of the different infrastructures relevant to SELECT and practical guidelines for the progress of the project.

Event Time Details
Team Meeting October 14, 2019 [19:00] Welcome aperitif and team building
Project Introduction October 15, 2019 [09:00] Welcoming remarks by Stefano Campostrini and presentation of the online project infrastructures by Daniele Durante [website, e-mail, GitHub repository, …]
Teams Presentation October 15, 2019 [09:20] Piemonte Orientale: Chiara Airoldi, Fabrizio Faggiano
Bocconi: Daniele Durante, Alessia Melagaro, Sirio Legramanti, Raffaella Piccarreta
Networking October 15, 2019 [11:00] Coffee break
Teams Presentation October 15, 2019 [11:15] Padova: Emanuele Aliverti, Mauro Bernardi, Stefano Mazzuco, Bruno Scarpa
Venice: Stefano Campostrini, Simone Gerzeli, Andrea Pastore, Stefano Tonellato, Lucia Zanotto, Francesca Zantomio
Networking October 15, 2019 [13:00] Lunch break at Trattoria Alle Due Gondolette
Goals of the Project October 15, 2019 [14:30] Each local unit discusses its own goals, thoughts and directions of research that will address as part of the project
Relevant Databases October 15, 2019 [15:30] Stefano Campostrini, Stefano Mazzuco and Fabrizio Faggiano present the available databases which could be relevant to the project [PASSI, SHARE, Human Mortality Database, WHO, …]
Networking October 15, 2019 [16:00] Coffee break
Concluding Remarks October 15, 2019 [16:30] Brainstorming, next appointments, networking, …
